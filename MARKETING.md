# Marketing & Distribution — Action Items

## Zapier & Make

- [ ] **Build Zapier app** — Create a proper Zapier integration app (not just webhook instructions). Submit to Zapier's app directory for review. Requires: trigger (screenshot completed webhook), action (take screenshot).
- [ ] **Build Make app** — Same as above for Make's app directory. Make's integration requires a custom module definition.

## Partnerships & Distribution

- [ ] **Reach out to comparison/review sites** — Contact authors of "best screenshot API" articles to include ScreenshotAPI in their reviews.
- [ ] **Developer newsletter sponsorships** — Consider sponsoring newsletters like TLDR, Bytes, JavaScript Weekly, or Python Weekly.
- [ ] **Build integrations with complementary tools** — Reach out to CMS platforms, monitoring tools, and no-code builders about native integrations.

## Ongoing

- [ ] **Monitor search rankings** — Track "screenshot API", "website screenshot API", "html to image API" and related keywords via Google Search Console.
- [ ] **Publish monthly blog posts** — Continue SEO content targeting long-tail screenshot API keywords. Aim for 4–8 posts per month.
- [ ] **Update competitor comparison pages quarterly** — Refresh pricing data, feature comparisons, and positioning as the market evolves.
- [ ] **Collect and publish customer testimonials** — Add social proof to landing page and comparison pages as customers adopt the product.
